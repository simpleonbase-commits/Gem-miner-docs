# The Forge — Upgrades

The Forge is where $GEM becomes power. Players spend $GEM to permanently upgrade three independent equipment trees. All upgrades are on-chain and persist to your wallet across sessions.

## Progression Path

A balanced approach is to upgrade all three trees together. Prioritize:

1. **Stone Pickaxe** — unlocks faster mining immediately (~45 min grind)
2. **Heart of the Mines** — extends stamina for longer dives
3. **Leather Armor** — survivability against early goblin pressure
4. Then alternate between pickaxe and stamina upgrades as you push deeper
