# Ghost Miner

The Ghost Miner is a rare spectral entity that appears only in the deepest parts of the world.

## Stats

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Minimum depth | 200m |
| Spawn chance | ~50% per check |
| Spawn cooldown | 10 seconds between attempts |
| Lifetime | 8 seconds |
| Movement | Unpredictable, every 700ms |
| $GEM reward | 25 |
| Score reward | 5,000 |
| Color | Spectral teal (`#00ffcc`) |

## How to Catch

The Ghost Miner moves erratically and disappears after 8 seconds. Move into it to catch it before it vanishes. It spawns 5–14 tiles from the player.

## Specter Upgrade

Players who hold the **Specter** upgrade gain passive bonuses that help with Ghost Miner hunting:

- **20% faster movement**
- **15% more rare gem finds**
