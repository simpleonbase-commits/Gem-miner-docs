# Mining & Ores

Nine ore types exist across the depth range. Each requires minimum pickaxe power to mine efficiently and yields $GEM on redemption.

## Ore Table

| Ore | Depth Range | $GEM per Block | Hardness |
|---|---|---|---|
| Coal | 10–130m | 0.25 | 15 |
| Copper | 15–155m | 0.50 | 20 |
| Iron | 25–210m | 1.25 | 25 |
| Silver | 35–260m | 3.75 | 30 |
| Gold | 50–310m | 10.00 | 40 |
| Diamond | 70–390m | 25.00 | 50 |
| Emerald | 80–420m | 50.00 | 60 |
| BAI Crystal | 100–470m | 125.00 | 80 |
| Void Ore | 300–510m | 250.00 | 110 |

## Artifacts

Three special artifact types appear at depth. These are rare collectibles that unlock achievement rewards:

- **Ancient Relic**
- **Crystal Prism**
- **Void Shard**

## Stamina

Mining consumes stamina. Deeper and harder ores cost more stamina per swing. Players must manage their stamina bar carefully on long dives — especially without upgrades. Upgrade your [Stamina Core](forge-upgrades.md#stamina) in the Forge to extend your mining sessions.

## Redemption

Ore is held in inventory until the player redeems it. Redemption mints real **$GEM tokens** directly to the connected wallet.

- **Minimum redemption:** 5 $GEM
- Unredeemed ore is lost on death — get to safety before you cash out

## Mining Rate (New Player)

A new player with a Wood Pickaxe mining coal at the surface earns approximately **10 $GEM per minute**. The first upgrade (Stone Pickaxe at 450 $GEM) is reachable in roughly **45 minutes** of uninterrupted play.
