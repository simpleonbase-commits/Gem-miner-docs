# Achievements

Twelve on-chain achievements can be unlocked permanently to your wallet. Each is earned once and awards a $GEM bonus.

| Achievement | Requirement | $GEM Reward |
|---|---|---|
| First Blood | Kill your first goblin | 2 |
| Ore Hoarder | Mine 100 blocks in one session | 2 |
| Combo Master | Reach a ×3 mining combo | 5 |
| Deep Diver | Reach 100m depth | 5 |
| Iron Will | Score 5,000 points without dying | 5 |
| Gold Rush | Mine 10 gold ores | 7 |
| Diamond Hands | Mine your first diamond | 7 |
| Darkness Dweller | Mine 20 blocks below 80m | 7 |
| Emerald Eye | Mine your first emerald | 7 |
| Goblin Slayer | Kill 10 goblins in one session | 10 |
| Crystal Hunter | Mine a BAI Crystal | 10 |
| Void Walker | Reach 450m depth | 12 |

Achievements are recorded on-chain and cannot be lost or reset.
