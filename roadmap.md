# Roadmap

Gem Miner is actively developed. Planned features include:

## Near Term
- Deeper world generation improvements
- Additional ore types and biomes
- Expanded achievement system

## Mid Term
- PvP gem stealing mechanics
- Seasonal leaderboards with $GEM prize pools
- Craftable items from mined artifacts
- Guild / party mining with shared reward pools

## Long Term
- Expanded world biomes and surface exploration
- Rare world events (cave-ins, lava surges, goblin raids)
- Land ownership mechanics
- Mobile-optimized controls
