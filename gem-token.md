# $GEM Token

$GEM is the native on-chain currency of Gem Miner.

## Token Details

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Name | GEM |
| Standard | ERC-20 |
| Chain | Base Mainnet |
| Chain ID | 8453 |
| Contract | `0xD3776969966B340d72d75731eF890A3Bc9F21bA3` |

## How to Earn

- **Mine ore** — redeem inventory for $GEM directly to your wallet
- **Kill goblins** — 1 $GEM per regular goblin, 3 $GEM per Alpha
- **Catch Ghost Miners** — 25 $GEM per catch (below 200m only)
- **Achievements** — one-time bonuses of 2–12 $GEM each

## How to Spend

$GEM is spent exclusively in the **[Forge](forge-upgrades.md)** to permanently upgrade your Pickaxe, Armor, and Stamina Core. Upgrades are on-chain and persist to your wallet across all sessions.

## Key Properties

- **Real on-chain asset** — no off-chain proxy points or intermediary currencies
- **Earned through play** — no presale, no airdrop farming
- **Spent in-game** — creates natural token sink through upgrades
